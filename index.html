About us!

Eugenia is a Russian translator originally from St. Petersburg (Russia, not Florida) who calls the Philadelphia area home. She was in Kent to get her Master's in Translation when she had the bright idea to meet a stranger for coffee... and the rest is (recent) history.

Nicholas is a software engineer (and math nerd) from Kent, who spends all his free time running crazy distances, scheming up new technologies, or talking about why the Midwest is great. He was working at a startup in Kent when he met Eugenia, cancelled his plans to leave the state, and stuck around to give this a shot.

Together they are forming the Tietz-Sokolskiye family, merging together into one long, unspellable behemoth.
